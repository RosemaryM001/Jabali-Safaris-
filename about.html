About Us

Established in 2015, Jabali Adventures & Safaris has risen to become one of the best in the tours and travelling in Kenya. Our CEO and founder, Mark Andrew Jabali, is passionate about tours and travels, and together with his dedicated team, they have changed the local tourism scene. At Jabali Adventures & Safaris, every client is highly valued, and we do not discriminate.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to ensuring that our clients get value for their money while enjoying safe and well-organized trips around the country.

Our Vision

To become the leading tours and traveling company in the East African region by offering well-designed and affordable packaged for our clients.

Our Commitment to You

We are commited to ensuring that we go beyond our clients' expectations with our afforadable packages while being keen to ensure that quality is not compromised. At Jabali Adventures and Safaris, we take customer feedback seriously, and we listen and respond to their needs, preferences and concerns.
